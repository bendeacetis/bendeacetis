airdrop
Project Details
airdrop is a software system that I made throughout a course at the University of Minnesota.
In this project, I worked to develop a working drone delivery service simulation that takes in a user specified pickup and drop off location, and then success fully has a drone pickup and deliver such package. In the program, this can be visualized.
Through this opportunity, I gained experience with design and developmental patterns. I learned fundamentals of software engineering as well as some exploration into javascript and html. The majority of my work was written in C++.
